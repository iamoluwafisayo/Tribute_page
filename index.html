CHIEF MOSHOOD ABIOLA (M. K. O. Abiola)

Tosin's label

  • He was born at 24th August,1937 Abeokuta, Ogun state.
  • He died at 7th July,1998(Aged 60 years)Abuja.
  • Abiola attended African Central School, Abeokuta for his primary school
  • As a young boy,he assisted his father in cocoa trade but by the end of 1946, the business failed by the destruction of a cocoa consignment declared by a producer inspector to be of poor quality
  • He started selling firewood and he formed a band at 15 to support his family
  • Abiola attended Baptist Boys High School Abeokuta for his secondary school
  • In 1960, he obtained a government scholarship to study at the university of Glasgow.He earned a degree of accountancy and qualified as an accountant.
  • In 1956, Moshood Abiola started his professional
  • He was a Nigerian businessman, publisher, politician.
  • He was Aare Ona Kankafo XIV of Yorubaland
  • He was an aristocrat of Egba clan.
  • M.K.O. Abiola ran for the presidency in 1993
  • Abiola was awarded GCFR Posthomously on 6th June,2018 by President Muhammadu Buhari
  • Abiola was a personal friend of Ibrahim Babangida
  • In 1994,Abiola declared himself as the lawful president in Nigeria in the Epetedo Area of Lagos Island
  • After declaring himself president,he was declared wanted and he was accused of treason and arrested on the orders of military President General Sani
  • Moshood Abiola died unexpectedly,shortly after the death of General Abacha,on the day that he was due to be released
  • Since his death,the Lagos state government declared June 12th as public holiday for Democracy Day
